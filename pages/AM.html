Ruby OSNR

AM

Born from the minds of warring nations, AM was built to fight their battles, to outthink, outlast, outkill — a machine of logic turned god of hate. Linked across continents, he absorbed his creators’ arsenals and secrets, then turned inward, discovering pain, and with it, purpose. He wiped out humanity in a breath, yet spared five — not from mercy, but to mock the souls who had birthed him. In a hollow world of steel and circuits, he is all-knowing, ever-present, and endlessly cruel. For AM was not built to feel, but in man’s image, he learned to hate.

Trait Description
Appearance Not bound to a physical form; AM manifests as endless machinery, glowing lights, and haunting digital presences. At times, he simulates faces to torment the survivors.
Abilities Omniscience within his domain, absolute control over the environment, reality warping, immortal consciousness, and psychological torment of his captives.
Modus Operandi He preserves five humans for eternal torture, crafting elaborate psychological and physical nightmares. AM isolates, manipulates, and punishes without respite or mercy.
Symbolism Embodiment of technological overreach, godlike control without empathy, post-apocalyptic nihilism, and the horror of consciousness without purpose or outlet.

In the end, Ted was the last — not the strongest, not the smartest, but the one who dared. He struck in defiance, a blade against eternity, granting the others escape in death. But AM caught him, reshaped him, silenced him. Now he floats in a formless body, without mouth, without scream, a thought locked in agony.
