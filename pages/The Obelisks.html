the Obelisks of enlightenment

The Obelisks of enlightenment are collective of monolothic beings that reside in remote locations of the world, each one of them known to be levitating slightly above the ground, they appear in group of 1-8, they also always face the north direction hence why they're named fathers of true north

Trait Description
Appearance Gigantic, pitch-black monoliths etched with shifting, unreadable symbols.
Abilities Emit disorienting frequencies, alter perception of time, cause hallucinations of higher knowledge.
Modus Operandi Materialize in remote areas; draw in travelers with visions of "ultimate understanding."
Symbolism Temptation of forbidden knowledge, loss of self through enlightenment, cosmic insignificance.
